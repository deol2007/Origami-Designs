Interesting Facts about Chameleon

  • Chamelons are reptiles that are part of the iguana suborder
  • Changing skin color is an importamt part of communication among chamleons

Interesting Facts about Camel

  • Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes.
  • Camels have thick lips which let them forage for thorny plants other animals can't eat.
  • There are two types of camels: One humped or “dromedary” camels and two humped Bactrian camels.

Interesting Facts about pigeon

  • Pigeons are one of only a small number of species to pass the 'mirror test' a test of self recognition.
  • Pigeons can also recognise each letter of the human alphabet
  • The rarest pigeon is the pink pigeon.

Interesting Facts about panda

  • Giant pandas have six fingers.
  • Pandas have 42 teeth most of which are flat crushing molars
  • Giant pandas can grow 2-3 feet tall.

Interesting Facts about cicada

  • Cicadas have redish orange eyes.
  • Cicadas make rather musical hums from their abdomens
  • Cidadas have three eyes.

Interesting Facts about bear

  • Bears care deeply for their family, and mother bears raise her one to two clubs until they can feed themselves.
  • Bears have excellent senses of smell, sight and hearing
